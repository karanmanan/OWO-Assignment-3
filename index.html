Project Description

‘Sports Hub’ is an application that assists people in finding various public sporting areas. The type of areas it will include will be, football ovals, soccer pitches, tennis courts and basketball courts and the App will be able to give you directions/information to each public sporting area. This is to promote the use of public sporting areas and get people to socialise through common interests. It does this by helping a user find Clubs and/or Coaches for their desired sports.

Joining a Club will allow you to be part of an official club that participates regularly and participant in entry level programs for each sport. You can find a club by a browse function where you search for it using a search bar that matches the words to appropriate teams or if a user is unsure but really wants to participate in a sport they can use a fun quiz inside the app in order to be matched to a club and a sport.

A user will be able to book appointments with coaches and book Indoor areas for their sport. When a user books a coach they can book them as a General inquiry where it would be similar to an introductory lesson where the user would get the general idea how and what will happen in their coaching sessions. They can book as a Personal training session where a user will be able to train 1-on-1 on fundamental skills of their chosen sports and Group training sessions where the user can train as part of a group and further enhance their Fundamentals and Team skills required for the sport.

A user can also create teams and join teams for various sports at a Beginner, Intermediate and Advanced level. The App also maintains a leaderboard similar to the likes of Uber where a user can give other users a star rating so people can spend the limited time they may have for a sport optimally. A user can use in-app messaging to talk to and maintain contacts that they have met through the app. Ultimately, participating in a sport ‘Sports Hub’ will make it easier to Enter and easier to gain more knowledge about any chosen sports that are featured on the App.

We would have to go through several phases to get to the result. If we consider Sports Hub, the product will go through at least seven stages of development. The stages of product development are as follows:

  1. Ideation and concept:
    Defining the product concept and the initial information or discussion/brainstorm where the product idea came from. This will help with having a clear vision of what is being produced and what the final outcome will roughly look like.

  2. Market research:
    Researching and studying the market to understand the user demands and current options available to users. By studying the market you can learn what the market demands and if there are any “gaps” in the market, meaning products that there is a need/demand for in the market but are not yet available. You can then try to fill that “gap” by creating a product that will meet those requirements and demands.

  3. Business plan:
    A plan will help with developing and marketing the product. It will also calculate at what point the business will become profitable and analyse the lifetime value of the product. This analysis can help with investments and resources put into creating the product according to the projected profit and value of the product.

  4. Prototype:
    Once you have a business plan a prototype would be ideal, as it can help test the product, can be shown to business partners/investors, used for marketing purposes, etc.

  5. Crowdfunding:
    Crowdfunding is a way to generate financial profits before the product is even finished. You can learn a lot by crowdfunding, such as getting first customer and investors feedback. This can help you gain more resources and knowledge about your customers before launching the finished product.

  6. Design and production:
    After crowdfunding you have received feedback and important market data from your first customers. This should help you create the design structure and start the production of your finished product. It could be creating databases for an application you want to create.

  7. Marketing and distribution:
    Now that the protection is finished and the product is launched, you need to advertise the product using social media, TV, ect. This will help you gain more profit and build customer loyalty, also will increase customers and demand of your product in the market.
